My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

A Man Called God

One of the best Korean shows I’ve ever seen. Song il-Guk has the best acting skills. A Man Called God tells a story of two children who got separated when they were young, the older ones who’s Michael witnessed his father getting murdered. And when he became older, he returned to his home country for revenge.
